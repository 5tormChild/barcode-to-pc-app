How do i put the scans on my spreadsheet (or other software)?

  1. Download Barcode to PC server from barcodetopc.com
  2. Install and start the server on your computer
  3. Open the Select server page from the app menu and choose your computer from the list
  4. Once the app is connected open your spreadsheet or another software of your choice in which you want to scan in, and position the cursor wherever you want to scan
  5. Scan your barcodes by tapping the camera icon on the main page of the app
  6. Thanks to the keyboard emulation, the barcodes will be typed in the cursor position

Connection problem/Unable to connect error

If it's the first time that you connect the app to the server your firewall or your antivirus may block the server, consult the Frequently Asked Questions.
